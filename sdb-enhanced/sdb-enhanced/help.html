SDB Enhanced

Getting Started

First, you'll need to visit each page of your SDB (click here).

Wait for "Queued ## items for processing." to appear at the top of the page (it should be nearly instant), then click Next until you've visited every page.

All of your items should now have been imported. Click the icon SDB to the right of your addressbar to open your new SDB.

Settings

Be sure to check the Settings Page (link).

Hover over the options to see information on them.

Note: if you do not enable the option "Lookup Rarities", only rarity estimates will be shown. (Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare/etc...)
